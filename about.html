About Us

Paramarsh is a dedicated environmental NGO committed to creating a sustainable future. We believe in the power of community action and strive to inspire individuals to take meaningful steps towards protecting our planet.

Our mission is to educate, engage, and empower people to make environmentally conscious decisions in their daily lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate, engage, and empower people to make environmentally conscious decisions in their daily lives. We aim to create a community of environmentally aware individuals who actively participate in conservation efforts.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone respects the environment and works together to preserve it for future generations. Our vision is to see a significant reduction in environmental degradation and a move towards sustainable living practices worldwide.

Meet Our Team

Our team is comprised of dedicated professionals from various backgrounds who share a common passion for the environment.

Our History

Paramarsh was founded in 2010 by a group of environmental enthusiasts who wanted to make a difference. Over the years, we have grown into a prominent organization with numerous successful projects under our belt.
